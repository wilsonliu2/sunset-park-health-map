Overview

Sunset Park is a neighborhood in Brooklyn with a population of ~131,654. It has a diverse demographic, with a significant portion of its residents being born outside the US and a high percentage of individuals with limited English proficiency.

Health outcomes in Sunset Park show that the community fares better than Brooklyn in terms of obesity and diabetes rates. However, hypertension remains a significant concern, and binge drinking rates are higher compared to the borough average.
